Chicken wings recipe

In this recipe we will make chicken wings. It takes about 30/45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken wings (or more)
  • microwave or oven

Steps

  1. Put the chicken wings on a plate.
  2. put it in oven or microwave
  3. cook the chicken wings
  4. eat them. Do this every day and you will be very healthy!
